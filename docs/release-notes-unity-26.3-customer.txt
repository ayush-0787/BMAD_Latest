RELEASE NOTES
================================================================================

Product:        TouchWorks EHR -- Unity
Version:        26.3
Release Date:   2026-07-06

This release improves patient safety by ensuring complete clinical data is
always surfaced in patient summaries, and expands mobile task management
with the ability to identify a patient's portal registration type before
sending tasks.

================================================================================
WHAT'S NEW
================================================================================

Identify Patient Portal Type Before Sending Mobile Tasks
  What it does:  When a patient is in context in TW Mobile, you can now
                 see whether the patient is registered through FMH or
                 Engage before initiating a task.
  Benefit:       Eliminates guesswork when routing tasks to patients --
                 you always know which portal the patient uses, so tasks
                 reach the right destination every time.
  How to access: Available in the TW Mobile task workflow whenever a
                 patient is in context. The patient's registration type
                 is displayed automatically -- no additional steps
                 required.

================================================================================
IMPROVEMENTS & RESOLVED ISSUES
================================================================================

Active Patient Allergies, Medications, and Problems Now Always Displayed
  What you may have noticed:  In some situations, a patient's active
                               allergies, medications, orders, or
                               verified problems were not appearing in
                               their clinical summary -- even though
                               those items were still active -- if they
                               had not been updated within a certain
                               time period.
  What's better now:          All active allergies, medications, orders,
                               and verified problems are now always
                               included in clinical summaries, regardless
                               of when they were last updated. You can be
                               confident that a patient's complete active
                               clinical picture is always visible.
