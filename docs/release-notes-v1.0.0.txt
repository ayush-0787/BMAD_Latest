NEW FEATURES
================================================================================

Single-Player Mode
  Description: Play solo against a 30-second countdown timer across 10 questions.
               Achieve 70% correct or better to win.
  Details:
    - Choose from two question categories: Politics or History.
    - 10 questions selected at random each round — no two games are identical.
    - 30-second timer per question; expiry counts as an incorrect answer.
    - Final screen displays your score and a Win (>=70% correct) or Lose verdict.

Pass-and-Play Multiplayer
  Description: Two to four players share one device and compete in turn. A
               handoff screen between turns keeps each player's answers private.
  Details:
    - Supports 2–4 players on a single Windows machine.
    - Each player enters their name before the round begins.
    - Players answer the same 10 questions independently, in sequence.
    - A handoff screen appears between turns so no player sees another's results.
    - Final leaderboard ranks players by percentage correct; tied players share
      the same rank.

Multi-Layered Scoring System
  Description: Scores reflect both accuracy and speed. Answering quickly and
               building answer streaks significantly increases your total.
  Details:
    - Correct answer: 100 base points.
    - Speed bonus: up to 100 additional points based on time remaining.
    - Streak multiplier: x1.0 (no streak), x1.5 (2 in a row), x2.0 (3-4 in a
      row), x3.0 (5 or more in a row). Streak resets on any wrong answer or
      timer expiry.

Question Banks: Politics and History
  Description: Two curated question categories are available at launch.
  Details:
    - Politics: questions drawn at random each round.
    - History: questions drawn at random each round.
