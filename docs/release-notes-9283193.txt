RTPB v13 -- Point-in-Time and Informational-Only Disclaimer Display
  Description: The Real-Time Prescription Benefit (RTPB) panel now displays
               a fixed disclaimer notifying prescribers that pricing and
               coverage information is a point-in-time estimate and that
               alternative medications are informational only.
  Details:
    - A disclaimer row has been added above the RTPB results grid.
    - Disclaimer text displayed to users:
        "Point-in-time estimate; final cost may change at the pharmacy.
         Alternatives are informational only."
    - Text is rendered in italics for visual distinction.
    - Applies to all RTPB v13 benefit displays within the Hub.
